  • SCOTIABANK WATCH AND WIN

    The Challenge

    Scotiabank has over 7.5 million personal and business customers across Canada. Scotiabank wanted to promote its Summer of FREE national movie tour and drive sign-ups for its SCENE debit and SCENE VISA cards.


    The Solution

    We gave Canadians the opportunity to vote for what movies would be shown for free in unique outdoor venues across the country. Over a 10-week span in the summer, Scotiabank posted movie questions in its Facebook Page. With facebook ads and promoted photo posts the contest generated more than 33,000 new Page likes and over 45,000 total contest entries. Showing incredibly strong daily engagement, contest entrants came back to engage with the promotion an average of 3.75 times during the campaign run.
